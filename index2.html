Tuition and Fees

Application Fee

The application fee for the 2023-2024 academic year is $2,760 ($2,400 plus 15% VAT) and must accompany each application. The application fee is non-refundable and non-deferrable. Please see payment instructions below.

New Student Enrollment Fee

Newly registered students will be charged a non-refundable New Student Registration Fee of $10,000 (excluding VAT). A new student registration fee will also apply if a student returns to ISG after an absence of more than three (3) years.

Installment plan fees

We offer the opportunity to pay the annual tuition fees in three (3) installments through the installment plan method. The cost associated with the installment plan is $2,000 (excluding VAT) per student per year.

Partial enrollment plan fees for the year

For students who will not attend the entire academic year, provided this is announced before the start of the academic year, a partial enrollment plan for the academic year is available at a cost of $2,000 (excluding VAT).

Elementary and Middle School

ASD provides a full American curriculum for its students that is standards-based. This model of unit and course design has been shown to be more innovative in terms of moving to increased student-centered classrooms focused on learner agency. Our continually updated curricular areas are designed with learner outcomes as our focus and excellent text materials are used as primary sources of information. Teachers have multiple supplementary resources available to help them effectively address the outcomes, including our outstanding library resources.
