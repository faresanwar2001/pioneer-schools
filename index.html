About Us

Pioneer Schools is a group of schools that originated in 2000
in most Arab countries and has achieved great success due to advanced curricula
and good dealings with all members of the organization, from the worker to the parents.
They are schools concerned with teaching foreign curricula and dealing well with children in order
to produce a generation that deals and cooperates with all members of society with their knowledge, behavior and skills.
